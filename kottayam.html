Illikal kallu

Illikkal Kallu is a Thalanadu located on top of the Illickal Malaa in the Kottayam district of Kerala, India.The distance from kottayam railway station to illikal kallu is 57km. Situated at around 3500 feet above sea level, Illickal Kallu is a major tourist attraction in Thalanadu. A trek for 3km from Erattupetta along the steep hilly road takes you to Illikkal Kallu and is the best viewpoint destination in the district. Post monsoon season (September to January) is the best time to visit this place. During monsoon the paths are slippery so it is risky

Marmala Waterfalls

Marmala waterfall is located in Teekoy village near Erattupetta in Kottayam district. The waterfall is a hidden treasure, 7 km away from Erattupetta town and 16 km away from Vagamon. Though not much popular, Marmala Waterfall offers a really attractive and pleasing view to the beholders, the tourists. The waterfall is about 60 meters in height, falling into a 12-meter deep pool and joins the river on way down. The falls are very dangerous and not recommended for taking bath but the river is an ideal place for taking bath. Marmala waterfall is a place for trekkers and adventure lovers, the dense jungle before the waterfall gives a trekking experience to its tourists. The waterfall offers splendid view of the natural beauty of an ideal waterfall.
